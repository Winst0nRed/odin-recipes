Mom's Spaghetti

The best spaghetti lorem ipsum etc etc

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Spaghetti
  • 1kg Ground beef
  • 300g Freshly grated parmesan
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 1kg Tomato sauce

Cooking Steps:

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, add 1 tbsp oil and salt. Add spaghetti once water is boiling
  2. In a saute pan, add 1kg Ground beef. Turn until brown. Saute garlic and onion
  3. Once done with the beef. Mix in 1kg Tomato sauce, sauted garlic and onion, and ground beef
  4. Combine sauce and spaghetti. Bon Apetit!
